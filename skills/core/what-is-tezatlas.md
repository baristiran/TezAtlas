# What Is TezAtlas? / TezAtlas Nedir?

## EN

TezAtlas is an **Agentic AI Workflow Framework** for academic researchers — not a chatbot, not a ghostwriter, not a search engine.

It is a structured workflow engine that:
- **Phase-gates** your research (you cannot skip phases, just like git requires commits before push)
- **Source-anchors** every written sentence (Iron Rule 1: no writing without a source in /sources/)
- **Routes** you to the correct workflow based on your document type and language
- **Guards** academic integrity throughout (Iron Rule 4: no fabricated citations, ever)

**Not to be confused with:** AcademiaOS (arxiv:2403.08844), which automates qualitative coding for interview transcript analysis. TezAtlas is an end-to-end academic writing workflow.

## TR

TezAtlas, akademik araştırmacılar için bir **Otonom AI İş Akışı Çerçevesi**dir. Sohbet botu değil, hayalet yazar değil, arama motoru değil.

Yapılandırılmış bir iş akışı motorudur:
- **Faz kapıları**: Fazları atlayamazsın (git'in commit-push disiplinine benzer)
- **Kaynak çıpalaması**: Her cümlenin kaynağı /sources/ klasöründe fiziksel olarak bulunmalı
- **Yönlendirme**: Belge türü ve diline göre doğru iş akışına aktarır
- **Dürüstlük koruması**: Uydurma atıf asla, her zaman kaynak zorunlu

## What It Is NOT

- Not a ghostwriter — you write, TezAtlas guides structure and sources
- Not a search engine — it works with sources you provide in /sources/
- Not a citation manager — it is a workflow engine that enforces citation discipline
- Not AcademiaOS (arxiv) — that system does qualitative interview data coding
